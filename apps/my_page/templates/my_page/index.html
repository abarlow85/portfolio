Donation Inventory Manager

This app was the result of a 4 week Scrum Project. I was part of the development team tasked with building an application for internal use by Good Karma Bikes, a non-profit organization based in San Jose, CA. The application allows staff and volunteers to check in and price a donation for re-sale, add it to the company’s Lightspeed Retail inventory system, and print a custom format label with a unique barcode. The application turned a 30 minute task into one that took less that 2 minutes to complete. Technologies used: Python, Django, AngularJS, Lightspeed Retail API, JsBarcode, HTML, CSS, Twitter Bootstrap, Responsive Design.

Roomies

Built by a team of three over four days, this is an iOS and web app allowing a household, or any group, to designate tasks with completion dates for users in the group. Both apps share the same back-end and database and both utilize sockets in order to instantly update users with new information, including a real-time chat room for each task. Technologies used: Swift, Xcode, JavaScript, Node.js, Express.js, Mongoose.js, Socket.io, AngularJS, HTML, CSS, MaterializeCSS.
